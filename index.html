Killington farmers market

july 10th 2020

- september 25th 2020

Every Friday through September 25, the Killington Farmers Market takes place at the new location - Killington Sports at Rt 4.

  • Attendees of the market should park behind Killington Sports @Rt4.
  • Please follow the suggested on-way flow of traffic through the mmarket.
  • In conjunction with Vermont's executive order and our resort policy, masks covering the nose and mouth are required at all times at the resort, both indoors and outdoors, except while on a bike or hiking trail, on the golf course, or seated while eating and drinking.
  • Most vendors will accept credit cards however some will be cash only. Please plan accordingly however there is an ATM onsite.
  • Don't forget your reusable grocery bag!
  • Restrooms are available inside Killington Sports at Rt4 - you will be required to wear a mask while utilizing them.

perks and discounts

Ikon Pass comes with 10 Friends and Family tickets and the Ikon Base Pass comes with 8 Friends and Family tickets (with holiday restrictions) for 25% off of the window rate at all Ikon Pass or Ikon Base Pass destinations.

Friends and Family tickets may be purchased from any open ticket window at Killington or Pico. Passholder must be present at the ticket window when requesting Bring a Friend tickets.

Ikon Pass holders receive 20% off select Killington lodging through Killington Central Reservations. Book online or call 800-621-MTNS to book over the phone.

first time at killington?

Your Ikon Pass gives your direct-to-lift access at both Killington and Pico, which means no waiting in ticket lines - just grab your gear and head straight to the lift. If you need your pass printed though, you'll need to stop in at Guest Services in either Snowshed or K-1 Base Lodges before hitting the hill. Visiting the biggest resort in Eastern North America means you’ll have access to more terrain than you can ski in a day, and it also means getting around on your first visit can be daunting. Lucky for you, we've gathered our top tips for making your first visit as easy as possible.

FIRST TIMERS GUIDE

consider pico mountain

Your Ikon Pass includes access to our sister resort, Pico Mountain, located just 7 miles down the road. Open Thursday-Monday (open Tuesdays and Wednesdays for Christmas and President’s weeks), Pico offers big mountain skiing with small mountain charm. Make the easy drive yourself or hop on the Killington Rutland commuter shuttle operated by The Bus.

Days scanned at Pico Mountain will count toward your allotted days at Killington/Pico. If you choose to ski at both Pico and Killington on the same day, it will only count as one day.

beast 365 pass details
  • The Beast 365 Pass is only available for purchase through October 15, 2020. After that date it will not be available for purchase again until spring 2021.
  • There are two options for purchasing this pass: Pay in full or subscribe to the monthly payments. You must opt in before July 15, 2020 to take advantage of the monthly payment option.
  • If you opt for monthly payments:
    • The first month's fee is due at time of purchase with the monthly fee being charged on the first day of each month thereafter beginning in July.
    • The monthly fee will continue indefinitely, purchasers will not need to sign up again next year.
    • 12-month commitment is required with a debit or credit card on file for monthly payments (no gift cards), cannot be canceled. To cancel after the first 12 months, email your request to seasonpass@killington.com or send it via mail. The full risk and billing agreement is available here.
  • If you opt to pay in full, you will need to re-purchase a pass again for next year.
  • The pass is valid from the start of Summer 2020 mountain biking season through the end of Winter 2020-21.
  • The included Ikon Base Pass does not provide access to Jackson Hole or Aspen Snowmass. There is no option to upgrade to the Ikon Base Plus Pass or full Ikon Pass.
  • To obtain your Ikon Base Pass: Beast 365 purchasers will receive a one-time code via email. Codes will be emailed on or after September 1, 2020 for redemption on ikonpass.com. If you purchase your Beast 365 Pass after September 1, you will receive your one-time code from us shortly after purchase. You must redeem your code on ikonpass.com by December 10, 2020.
    • After completing your code redemption, you will be placed in the queue to receive your Ikon Base Pass along with all other Ikon Pass purchasers (mailing begins in September 2020). The Ikon Pass system will not allow us to print your pass for you at Killington/Pico. You will need to receive it in the mail or pick it up from another Ikon resort.
    • Once you redeem your Ikon Base Pass, you become ineligible to cancel your pass under the Pass Holder Promise.
    • The included Ikon Base Pass is not eligible for the Adventure Assurance program.
